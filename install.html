add-listing — bookmarklet installer

Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar, then click it on any eBay listing page. It will open a pre-filled form where you can review the title, description URL, and BIN before saving.

⬆ Drag me to bookmarks bar

Step 1 — Make sure bookmarks are enabled

Step 2 — Drag and drop the bookmark to your bookmarks bar. Name it whatever you like.

Step 3 — On a listing you wish to back up, click the bookmark you saved.
